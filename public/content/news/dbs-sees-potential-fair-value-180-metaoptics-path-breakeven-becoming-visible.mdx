  <div className="mdx-text">
  In a recent article published by The Edge Singapore, citing a research note by DBS Group Research analyst Ling Lee Keng, MetaOptics was highlighted for its semiconductor-style, wafer-level manufacturing approach, which differentiates it from traditional glass-lens manufacturers and enables scalable production of ultra-thin optical components for high-volume consumer electronics and automotive applications.
  </div>
  
  <div className="mdx-text">
  The article notes that this manufacturing model addresses a key industry bottleneck, supporting consistent output of up to 30 million lenses annually and positioning the technology for mass-market adoption. With development agreements secured and active sampling underway with leading consumer electronics players, MetaOptics is viewed as well positioned to support production scale-up and commercial adoption.
  </div>

  <div className="mdx-text">
  Ling also points to collaborations with NVIDIA and Qualcomm as factors that may facilitate integration into the mainstream product supply chain.
