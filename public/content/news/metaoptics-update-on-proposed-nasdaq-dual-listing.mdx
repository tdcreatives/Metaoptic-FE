  <div className="mdx-text">
    <b>MetaOptics Ltd (Catalist: 9MT) ("MetaOptics" or the "Company", and together with its subsidiaries, the "Group")</b>

    under the Securities Act of 1933 of the United States, as amended, relating to a proposed offering of ADSs in
